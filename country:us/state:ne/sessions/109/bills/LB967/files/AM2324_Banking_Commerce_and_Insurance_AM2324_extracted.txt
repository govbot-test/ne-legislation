11 Sec. 15. Sections 15 to 24 of this act shall be known and may be
12 cited as the Nebraska Protection of Seniors from Insurance Exploitation
13 Act.
14 Sec. 16. (1) It is the intent of the Legislature to provide legal
15 protection to insurers and insurance producers so that they have the
16 discretion to take action to assist in detecting and preventing financial
17 exploitation.
18 (2) The Legislature recognizes that insurers and insurance producers
19 are in a unique position to potentially discover financial exploitation
20 when conducting transactions on behalf of and at the request of their
21 customers.
22 (3) The Legislature recognizes that insurers and insurance producers
23 have duties imposed by contract and duties imposed by both federal and
24 state law to conduct transactions requested by their customers faithfully
25 and timely in accordance with the customer's instructions.
26 (4) The Legislature recognizes that insurers and insurance producers
27 do not have a duty to contravene the valid instructions of their
28 customers and nothing in the Nebraska Protection of Seniors from
29 Insurance Exploitation Act creates such a duty.
