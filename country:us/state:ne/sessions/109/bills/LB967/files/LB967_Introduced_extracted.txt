5 Sec. 2. (1) A mutual insurance holding company shall provide notice
6 of its annual meeting to its members as provided by the company's bylaws
7 or, if the bylaws are silent, in a manner that is reasonable. Electronic
8 notice, either by direct electronic transmission, publication on a
9 designated website, or comparable technology is reasonable if:
10 (a) For notice by direct electronic transmission, the notice is sent
11 to an electronic address or account that the member has designated for
12 receipt of notices or to an electronic address the company reasonably
13 believes will reach the recipient; or
14 (b) For notice published on a designated website, the company has
15 previously informed its members of the time, location, and procedure for
16 accessing notices published on a designated website.
17 (2) The notice described in subsection (1) of this section shall
18 include all information otherwise required by law, including instructions
19 for accessing meeting materials and for voting or participating in the
20 meeting if electronic participation or voting is permitted.
21 (3) Notice shall be effective on the date that such notice is sent,
22 or if published on a designated website, on the date the notice is
23 published. The company shall demonstrate delivery to the electronic
24 address or account or publication on a designated website, by
25 contemporaneous logs, delivery receipts, or portal access records.
26 (4) Nothing in this section precludes a company from using
27 electronic means or publication on its website to provide meeting
28 materials, proxies, ballots, or other member communications if the
29 company complies with the requirements of this section.
