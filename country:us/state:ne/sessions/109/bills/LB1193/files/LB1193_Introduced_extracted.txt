12 (a) The nameplate capacity tax established in 2010 has succeeded at

16 similar tax revenue, support economic development in the state, and

19 investment in the state and economic development; and
20 (c) To benefit the citizens of Nebraska, energy storage resources
21 should be subject to the nameplate capacity tax and protected from any
22 foreign adversary's control.
