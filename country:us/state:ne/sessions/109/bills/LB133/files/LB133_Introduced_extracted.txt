1 Section 1. Section 28-1008, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
2 2024, is amended to read:
3 28-1008 For purposes of sections 28-1008 to 28-1017, 28-1019, and
4 28-1020:
5 (1) Abandon means to leave any animal in one's care, whether as
6 owner or custodian, for any length of time without making effective
7 provision for its food, water, or other care as is reasonably necessary
8 for the animal's health;
9 (2) Animal means any vertebrate member of the animal kingdom. Animal
10 does not include an uncaptured wild creature or a livestock animal as
11 defined in section 54-902;
12 (3) Cruelly mistreat means to knowingly and intentionally kill,
13 maim, disfigure, torture, beat, kick, hit, strike in any manner,
14 mutilate, burn, scald, or otherwise inflict harm upon any animal;
15 (4) Cruelly neglect means to fail to provide any animal in one's
16 care, whether as owner or custodian, with food, water, or other care as
17 is reasonably necessary for the animal's health;
18 (5) Humane killing means the destruction of an animal by a method
19 which causes the animal a minimum of pain and suffering;
20 (6) Law enforcement officer means any member of the Nebraska State
21 Patrol, any county or deputy sheriff, any member of the police force of
22 any city or village, or any other public official or animal control
23 officer authorized by a city, or village, or county to enforce state or
24 local animal control laws, rules, regulations, resolutions, or
25 ordinances. Law enforcement officer also includes a special investigator
26 appointed as a deputy state sheriff as authorized pursuant to section
27 81-201 while acting within the authority of the Director of Agriculture
28 under the Commercial Dog and Cat Operator Inspection Act;
29 (7) Mutilation means intentionally causing permanent injury,
30 disfigurement, degradation of function, incapacitation, or imperfection
31 to an animal. Mutilation does not include conduct performed by a
