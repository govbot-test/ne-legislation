1 Section 1. (1) The Clerk of the Legislature is authorized to
2 distribute the session laws, the journal of the Legislature, and the
3 statutes of the state. The session laws, journal, and statutes may be
4 published in print or electronic format or in both formats.
5 (2) The session laws and journal shall be compiled and published by
6 the Clerk of the Legislature after each regular session of the
7 Legislature. The session laws shall contain all the laws passed by the
8 preceding session as well as those passed during any special session
9 since the last regular session. The session laws shall also contain a
10 certified copy of the Constitution of Nebraska as required by section
11 49-1701. The clerk shall distribute one copy of the session laws and the
12 journal to each member of the Legislature and shall distribute a second
13 copy to any member of the Legislature upon such member's request.
14 (3) The Revisor of Statutes shall cause the statutes to be printed
15 pursuant to section 49-704 and the policy described in subsection (4) of
16 this section. The printer shall deliver all completed copies to the Clerk
17 of the Legislature for distribution.
18 (4) The Clerk of the Legislature shall provide copies of the session
19 laws, journal, and statutes for distribution to judges, county officers
20 and offices, state officers and agencies, libraries, and other persons
21 pursuant to a policy adopted by the Executive Board of the Legislative
22 Council. The clerk shall distribute such copies by the most expeditious
23 and economic means as the clerk determines pursuant to the policy adopted
24 by the executive board. Any person may send a request to the clerk for
25 printed copies of the session laws, journal, or statutes and shall pay
26 the cost of production and delivery of such copies as such costs are
27 determined by the clerk based on prices prescribed by the executive board
28 pursuant to the policy. The clerk may sell or otherwise dispose of copies
29 determined to be surplus or superseded pursuant to the policy.
