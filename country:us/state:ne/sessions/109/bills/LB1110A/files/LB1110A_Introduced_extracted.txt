LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA

SECOND SESSION
Introduced by von Gillern, 4.

1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 1110, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026; to provide an operative
4 date; and to declare an emergency.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB1110A LB1110A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $105,300 from the
2 Department of Revenue Enforcement Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $-0- from
3 the Department of Revenue Enforcement Fund for FY2027-28 to the
4 Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in carrying out the
5 provisions of Legislative Bill 1110, One Hundred Ninth Legislature,
6 Second Session, 2026.
7 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
8 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
9 section.
10 Sec. 2. This act becomes operative on July 1, 2026.
11 Sec. 3. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
12 passed and approved according to law.
