1 Section 1. Sections 1 to 5 of this act shall be known and may be
2 cited as the Developmental Disabilities Provider Excessive Training and
3 Cost Reduction Act.
4 Sec. 2. The purpose of the Developmental Disabilities Provider
5 Excessive Training and Cost Reduction Act is to prohibit the department
6 from imposing unnecessarily costly training requirements on employees of
7 providers and to ensure that training requirements imposed by the
8 department are focused solely on ensuring the health, safety, and welfare
9 of the individuals served by providers. The Developmental Disabilities
10 Provider Excessive Training and Cost Reduction Act further ensures that
11 such training requirements are appropriate, competitively priced, and
12 meet all federal requirements for the provision of developmental
13 disabilities services.
14 Sec. 3. For purposes of the Developmental Disabilities Provider
15 Excessive Training and Cost Reduction Act:
16 (1) Department means the Department of Health and Human Services;
17 (2) Employee means an individual employed by a provider, including
18 full-time, part-time, temporary, and contract staff; and
19 (3) Provider means an individual, organization, or entity licensed
20 or certified by the state to provide services to individuals with
21 developmental disabilities.
22 Sec. 4. (1) The department shall not require, by rule, regulation,
23 or other guidance or directive, that:
24 (a) An employee of a provider complete training in the use of a
25 physical restraint unless such employee's job duties may reasonably
26 require the employee to physically restrain an individual with
27 developmental disabilities during the course of employment; and
28 (b) Physical restraint training, or related requirements for an
29 employee of a provider, exceed the minimum requirements necessary to
30 comply with applicable federal law or regulations.
31 (2) If the department requires physical restraint training, by rule,
