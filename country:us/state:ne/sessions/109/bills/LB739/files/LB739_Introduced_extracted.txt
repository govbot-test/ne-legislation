11 notice of the filing of the petition shall be published in a newspaper in

17 of age at the time the action is filed.
18 (b) The court may waive the notice requirement of subdivision (2)(a)
19 of this section upon a showing by the petitioner that such notice would
20 endanger the petitioner.
21 (3) In an action involving a petitioner under nineteen years of age
22 who has a noncustodial parent, notice of the filing of the petition shall

25 court pursuant to subsection (1) of section 42-364.13 for the
26 noncustodial parent if he or she has provided an address. The clerk of
27 the district court shall provide the petitioner with the address upon

30 satisfied by proof in open court of the truth of the allegations set
