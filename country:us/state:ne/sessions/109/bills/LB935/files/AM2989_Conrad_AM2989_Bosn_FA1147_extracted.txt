19 (c) Recipients that practice in a county with a population of no
20 more than seventy-five thousand inhabitants.
21 (3) An individual that has been awarded a loan prior to the
22 operative date of this section shall not be adversely affected by the
23 changes made to this section by this legislative bill and shall remain
24 eligible to retain such loan under the terms originally awarded.
