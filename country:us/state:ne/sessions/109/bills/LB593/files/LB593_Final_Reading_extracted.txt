1 Section 1. Section 66-1226, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 66-1226 (1) The Legislature hereby adopts by reference the ASTM
4 International American Society For Testing and Materials publication
5 D4814-24b D4814-89 entitled Standard Specification Specifications for
6 Automotive Spark-Ignition Spark Ignition Engine Fuel Fuels. The
7 Department of Agriculture shall file copies of such publication with the
8 Secretary of State and Clerk of the Legislature.
9 (2) Commencing on the effective date of this act January 1, 1992,
10 all automotive spark-ignition spark ignition engine fuel fuels sold in
11 Nebraska shall meet the specification as found in the ASTM International
12 American Society For Testing and Materials publication D4814-24b D4814-89
13 entitled Standard Specification Specifications for Automotive Spark-
14 Ignition Spark Ignition Engine Fuel Fuels. Any person who violates this
15 subsection shall be guilty of a Class I misdemeanor for the first such
16 violation and a Class IV felony for all subsequent violations.
17 (3) For purposes of this section, automotive spark-ignition spark
18 ignition engine fuel fuels shall mean gasoline and its blends with
19 oxygenates such as alcohol and ethers.
20 Sec. 2. Original section 66-1226, Reissue Revised Statutes of
21 Nebraska, is repealed.
