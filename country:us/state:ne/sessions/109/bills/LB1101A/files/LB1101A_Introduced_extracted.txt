1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 1101, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026; and to declare an
4 emergency.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB1101A LB1101A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $-0- from the Judges
2 Expense Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $7,500 from the Judges Expense Fund
3 for FY2026-27 to the Public Employees Retirement Board, for Program 41,
4 to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 1101, One
5 Hundred Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
6 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
7 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
8 section.
9 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
