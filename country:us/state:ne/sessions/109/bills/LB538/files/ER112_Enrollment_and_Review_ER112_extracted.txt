E AND R AMENDMENTS TO LB538
1 1. On page 1, strike lines 2 through 12 and insert "85-1411, Reissue
2 Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to require the State Board of Education to
3 develop and distribute a model policy relating to discrimination,
4 including antisemitism, as prescribed; to require each school board to
5 adopt a policy relating to discrimination, including antisemitism, as
6 prescribed; to require antisemitism awareness training as prescribed; to
7 require the designation of a Title VI coordinator within the State
8 Department of Education and provide powers and duties; to require the
9 Coordinating Commission for Postsecondary Education to designate a Title
10 VI coordinator as prescribed and provide powers and duties; to state
11 intent relating to postsecondary educational institutions adopting
12 policies relating to antisemitism; to harmonize provisions; and to repeal
13 the original sections.".
