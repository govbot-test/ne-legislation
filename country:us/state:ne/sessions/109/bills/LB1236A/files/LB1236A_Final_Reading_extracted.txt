1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 1236, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026; and to declare an
4 emergency.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB1236A LB1236A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $25,000 from the
2 Nebraska Statutes Distribution Cash Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $25,000
3 from the Nebraska Statutes Distribution Cash Fund for FY2027-28 to the
4 Legislative Council, for Program 123, to aid in carrying out the
5 provisions of Legislative Bill 1236, One Hundred Ninth Legislature,
6 Second Session, 2026.
7 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
8 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
9 section.
10 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
11 passed and approved according to law.
