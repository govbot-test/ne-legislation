8 Sec. 11. It is the intent of the Legislature that state agencies
9 may, at their discretion, utilize employee furloughs as a short-term
10 means of addressing budgetary shortfalls. Furlough is defined as placing
11 an employee in a temporary, nonduty, nonpay status because of the lack of
12 funds. An intermittent furlough is a furlough action in which the
13 nonduty, nonpay status occurs discontinuously over a period of time
14 rather than consecutively. It is further intended that furloughs shall
15 not adversely affect the employee and employer health insurance premium
16 contributions and service anniversary date, nor shall leave earnings be
17 prorated as a result of the furlough.
18 Sec. 12. It is the intent of the Legislature that the
19 appropriations for capital facilities follow an orderly and reasonable
20 process based upon defined and documented need and an analysis of the
21 utilization of existing facilities.
