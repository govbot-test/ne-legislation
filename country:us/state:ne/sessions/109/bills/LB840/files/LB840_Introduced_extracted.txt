1 Section 1. Section 58-701, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025, is
2 amended to read:
3 58-701 Sections 58-701 to 58-712 and section 2 of this act shall be
4 known and may be cited as the Nebraska Affordable Housing Act.
5 Sec. 2. (1) For purposes of this section:
6 (a) Accessible has the same meaning as in 24 C.F.R. 8.3, as such
7 regulation existed on January 1, 2026; and
8 (b) Multifamily housing project means a project involving the
9 construction of one or more residential structures that, as a whole,
10 contain five or more dwelling units.
11 (2) Beginning on the effective date of this act, the Department of
12 Economic Development shall not approve a multifamily housing project for
13 assistance from the Affordable Housing Trust Fund unless:
14 (a) At least twenty percent of the dwelling units included in the
15 project will be accessible for persons with mobility impairments; and
16 (b) At least ten percent of the dwelling units included in the
17 project will be accessible for persons with hearing or vision
18 impairments.
19 Sec. 3. Original section 58-701, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025,
20 is repealed.
