1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to public health and welfare; to amend section
2 71-2451.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change
3 provisions relating to management of long-term care facilities; and
4 to repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB1239 LB1239
2026 2026
1 Section 1. Section 71-2451.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska,
2 is amended to read:
3 71-2451.01 Unless otherwise allowed by state or federal law or
4 regulation, the management of a long-term care facility at which an
5 automated medication system is located shall not require a resident of
6 the facility to obtain medication through the automated medication system
7 and shall not restrict or impair the ability of a resident of the
8 facility to obtain a medication medications from the pharmacy of the
9 resident's choice.
10 Sec. 2. Original section 71-2451.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of
11 Nebraska, is repealed.
