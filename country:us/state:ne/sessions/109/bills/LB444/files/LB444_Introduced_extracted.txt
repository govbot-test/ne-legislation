LEGISLATIVE BILL 444
Introduced by Dungan, 26.

Committee: Transportation and Telecommunications
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to transportation; to amend section 75-307,
2 Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement, 2024; to require certain
3 minimum uninsured and underinsured insurance coverage per occupant
4 for certain certificated intrastate motor carriers, certain motor
5 carriers transporting household goods, and any motor carrier
6 transporting employees of a railroad carrier; and to repeal the
7 original section.
8 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
