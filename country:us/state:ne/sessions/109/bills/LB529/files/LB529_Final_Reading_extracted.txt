3 73-803 For purposes of the State Procurement Act:
4 (1) Contract includes any contract for services and contract for

22 contemplated in the agreement;
23 (5) Division means the materiel division of the Department of
24 Administrative Services;
25 (6) Emergency means necessary to meet an urgent or unexpected
26 requirement or when health and public safety or the conservation of
27 public resources is at risk;
28 (7) Grant agreement means a legal instrument reflecting a
