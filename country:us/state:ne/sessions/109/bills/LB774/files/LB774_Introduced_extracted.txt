1 Section 1. (1)(a) The Rural Health Transformation Program Fund is
2 created. The fund shall be administered by the Department of Health and
3 Human Services and shall consist of money received from the federal
4 Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services for the Rural Health
5 Transformation Program. Money in the fund shall be used to support
6 communities in transforming health care delivery systems and improving
7 access to health care and health outcomes.
8 (b) Any money in the Rural Health Transformation Program Fund
9 available for investment shall be invested by the state investment
10 officer pursuant to the Nebraska Capital Expansion Act and the Nebraska
11 State Funds Investment Act.
12 (2) The department shall electronically report to the Legislature on
13 or before December 15 of each year regarding the use of the fund and the
14 outcomes achieved from the use of the fund.
