1 Section 1. Each full-time employee of a school district shall be
2 allowed five days of mental health leave each school fiscal year. The
3 employee shall be paid at the employee's usual salary in full for any
4 mental health leave used by the employee. Mental health leave shall not
5 count against any other leave the employee accrues as a result of working
6 for such school district. A school district shall not require or request
7 any documentation from a medical or mental health provider to justify the
8 use of mental health leave.
9 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
