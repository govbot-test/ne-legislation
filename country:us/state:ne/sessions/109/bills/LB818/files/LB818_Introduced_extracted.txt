19 (c) The person has been previously convicted:
20 (i) Of of a violation of this section; or .

22 essentially the same elements as a violation of this section.
23 (5) It is an affirmative defense that an act constituting
24 strangulation or suffocation was the result of a legitimate medical
25 procedure.
