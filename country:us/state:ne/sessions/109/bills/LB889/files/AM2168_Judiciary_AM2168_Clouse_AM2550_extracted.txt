17 rule, regulation, or order of the board.
18 (2) Subdivision (1)(b) of this section shall not apply to a person
19 performing paid electrical work for such person's parent, stepparent,
20 spouse, descendant, grandparent, brother, sister, cousin, uncle, or aunt,
21 whether of the whole or half blood or by adoption or marriage.
