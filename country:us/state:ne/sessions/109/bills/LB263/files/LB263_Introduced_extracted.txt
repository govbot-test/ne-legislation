1 Section 1. APPROPRIATION LANGUAGE.
2 There are hereby appropriated, for FY2025-26 and FY2026-27, the
3 amounts specified in this act, or so much as may be necessary, for the
4 salaries and benefits of officers of the Nebraska state government.
5 Sec. 2. DEFINITION OF APPROPRIATION PERIOD.
6 For purposes of this act, FY2025-26 means the period beginning July
7 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026, and FY2026-27 means the period
8 beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027.
9 Sec. 3. NEBRASKA ACCOUNTING SYSTEM MANUAL DEFINITIONS.
10 The definitions contained in the Nebraska Accounting System Manual,
11 and any amendments thereto, on file with the Clerk of the Legislature are
12 hereby adopted by the Legislature as the definitions for this act, except
13 as provided in section 5 of this act.
14 Sec. 4. DRAWING AND PAYING WARRANTS.
15 The Director of Administrative Services shall draw warrants upon the
16 proper fund in the state treasury for an amount not to exceed the
17 appropriations set forth in this act upon presentation of proper
18 documentation. The State Treasurer shall pay the warrants out of the
19 appropriate funds.
20 Workers' compensation premium assessments shall not be paid from the
21 programs listed in this act.
22 Sec. 5. LIMITATION ON SALARIES, WAGES, AND PER DIEMS.
23 (1) As used in this act:
24 (a) SALARY LIMIT means total expenditures for permanent and
25 temporary salaries and per diems; and
26 (b) Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
27 diems means all remuneration paid to employees treated as taxable
28 compensation by the Internal Revenue Service or subject to social
29 security coverage, specifically including payments accounted for as
30 vacation, holidays, sick leave, military leave, funeral leave, maternity
31 leave, administrative leave, compensatory time, deferred compensation, or

2 excluding state contributions for social security, retirement, and

4 (2) Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
