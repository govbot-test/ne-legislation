LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
SECOND SESSION
Introduced by Education Committee: Murman, 38, Chairperson; Hughes, 24;
Juarez, 5; Lonowski, 33; Meyer, G., 17; Sanders, 45.
Read first time January 14, 2026
Committee: Education
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to schools; to amend section 79-257, Reissue
2 Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change provisions relating to the
3 Student Discipline Act; to harmonize provisions; and to repeal the
4 original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB1052 LB1052
2026 2026
1 Section 1. Section 79-257, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 79-257 The school board or board of education may authorize the
4 emergency exclusion, short-term or long-term suspension, expulsion, or
5 mandatory reassignment of any student pupil from school for conduct
6 prohibited by the board's rules or standards established pursuant to the
7 Student Discipline Act if such emergency exclusion, short-term or long-
8 term suspension, expulsion, or mandatory reassignment complies with the
9 procedures required by the act.
10 Sec. 2. Original section 79-257, Reissue Revised Statutes of
11 Nebraska, is repealed.
-2-
