LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
SECOND SESSION
Introduced by Riepe, 12.
Read first time January 07, 2026
Committee: General Affairs
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the Nebraska County and City Lottery Act;
2 to amend section 9-648, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to
3 change the percentage of the tax required on gross proceeds under
4 the act; to provide an operative date; to repeal the original
5 section; and to declare an emergency.
6 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB782 LB782
2026 2026
1 Section 1. Section 9-648, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 9-648 Any county, city, or village which conducts a lottery shall
4 submit to the department on a quarterly basis a tax of one two percent of
5 the gross proceeds. Such tax shall be remitted not later than thirty days
6 from the close of the preceding quarter on forms provided by the
7 department. The department shall remit the tax to the State Treasurer for
8 credit to the Charitable Gaming Operations Fund. All deficiencies of the
9 tax imposed by this section shall accrue interest and be subject to a
10 penalty as provided for sales and use taxes in the Nebraska Revenue Act
11 of 1967.
12 Sec. 2. This act becomes operative on July 1, 2026.
13 Sec. 3. Original section 9-648, Reissue Revised Statutes of
14 Nebraska, is repealed.
15 Sec. 4. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
16 passed and approved according to law.
