LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 283CA
PROPOSED CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT
Introduced by DeKay, 40; Andersen, 49; Clements, 2; Hardin, 48;
Holdcroft, 36; Lippincott, 34; Murman, 38.
Read first time January 07, 2026
Committee: Government, Military and Veterans Affairs
1 THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, SECOND
2 SESSION, RESOLVE THAT:
3 Section 1. At the general election in November 2026, the following
4 proposed amendment to the Constitution of Nebraska shall be submitted to
5 the electors of the State of Nebraska for approval or rejection:
6 To amend Article VI, section 1:
7 VI-1 Only a Every citizen of the United States who has attained the
8 age of eighteen years on or before the first Tuesday after the first
9 Monday in November and has resided within the state and the county and
10 voting precinct for the terms provided by law shall, except as provided
11 in section 2 of this article, be an elector for the calendar year in
12 which such citizen has attained the age of eighteen years and for all
13 succeeding calendar years.
14 Sec. 2. The proposed amendment shall be submitted to the electors
15 in the manner prescribed by the Constitution of Nebraska, Article XVI,
16 section 1, with the following ballot language:
17 A constitutional amendment to state that only a United States
18 citizen may be an elector.
19 For
20 Against.
