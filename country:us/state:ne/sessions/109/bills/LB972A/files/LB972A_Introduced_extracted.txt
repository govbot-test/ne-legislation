LB972A LB972A
2026 2026
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
SECOND SESSION
Introduced by Fredrickson, 20.
Read first time March 25, 2026
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 972, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB972A LB972A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $72,400 from the
2 General Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $69,400 from the General Fund for
3 FY2027-28 to the Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in
4 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 972, One Hundred Ninth
5 Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $50,700
8 for FY2026-27 or $52,200 for FY2027-28.
9 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $13,500 from the Military
10 Department Cash Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $30,000 from the Military
11 Department Cash Fund for FY2027-28 to the Military Department, for
12 Program 544, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill
13 972, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
14 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2026-27
15 $13,500 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
16 purpose. There is included in the appropriation to this program for
17 FY2027-28 $30,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for
18 such purpose.
19 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
20 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
21 section.
22 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated (1) $520,700 from the License
23 Plate Cash Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $528,900 from the License Plate
24 Cash Fund for FY2027-28 to the Department of Motor Vehicles, for Program
25 90, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 972, One
26 Hundred Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
27 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
28 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
29 section.
