LB815A LB815A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $137,300 from the Motor
2 Fuel Tax Enforcement and Collection Cash Fund for FY2026-27 and (2)
3 $136,300 from the Motor Fuel Tax Enforcement and Collection Cash Fund for
4 FY2027-28 to the Department of Revenue, for Program 111, to aid in
5 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 815, One Hundred Ninth
6 Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
7 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
8 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $99,500
9 for FY2026-27 or $102,500 for FY2027-28.
