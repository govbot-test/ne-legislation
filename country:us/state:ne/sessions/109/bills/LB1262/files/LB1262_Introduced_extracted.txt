6 Sec. 4. (1) No postsecondary institution, or any affiliate
7 organization, may accept, solicit, or enter into, directly or indirectly,
8 a gift, grant, contract, or funding, a research sponsorship, a program
9 agreement, a partnership, or any other funding relationship with a
10 foreign adversary or foreign principal.
11 (2) Funds accepted in violation of this section shall be returned or
12 forfeited to the State Treasurer to be credited as required by Article
13 VII, section 7, of the Constitution of Nebraska.
14 (3) Cultural exchange agreements are prohibited with a foreign
15 adversary or any entity controlled by a foreign adversary unless the
16 postsecondary institution certifies in writing to the Coordinating
17 Commission for Postsecondary Education that the agreement in no way
18 constrains freedom of speech, controls curricular materials, or promotes
19 an agenda detrimental to the safety of this state or the United States.
20 (4) No student or scholar association affiliated with or operating
21 at any postsecondary institution may coordinate activities with a foreign
22 adversary or any entity controlled by a foreign adversary. Any violation
23 of this prohibition shall result in the postsecondary institution ending
24 any affiliation with the student or scholar association.
