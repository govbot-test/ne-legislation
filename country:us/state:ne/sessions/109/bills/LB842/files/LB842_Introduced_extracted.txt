1 Section 1. (1) Any city of the first class, city of the second
2 class, or village may enter into contracts or agreements with any person,
3 firm, corporation, or political subdivision for the operation,
4 maintenance, management, or enforcement of municipal parking facilities,
5 including onstreet metered parking and offstreet public lots or garages.
6 (2) Such contracts or agreements may authorize the person, firm,
7 corporation, or political subdivision to issue and process parking
8 citations, collect fines, maintain equipment, or operate payment systems,
9 provided that the city or village retains final authority over
10 adjudication and appeals.
11 (3) The city or village shall retain sufficient control over the
12 parking facilities to ensure proper operation in the public interest and
13 to establish or approve all rates, charges, and fines.
14 (4) No contract or agreement under this section shall exceed:
15 (a) Thirty years in duration, including renewals, for a city of the
16 first class; or
17 (b) Ten years in duration, including renewals, for a city of the
18 second class or village.
19 Sec. 2. The Revisor of Statutes shall assign section 1 of this act
20 to Chapter 19.
