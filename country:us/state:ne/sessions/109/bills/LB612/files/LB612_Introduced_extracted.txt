1 Section 1. Section 24-514, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 24-514 (1) The State of Nebraska shall pay, with funds appropriated
4 to the Supreme Court, all salaries, benefits, and expenses related to the
5 education and travel of judges and employees of the county courts. The
6 state shall also pay, with funds appropriated to the Supreme Court, fifty
7 percent of the following operational costs of the county courts. :
8 (1) Computer hardware and software used for data processing;
9 (2) Computer hardware and software used for word processing if the
10 costs are incurred on equipment owned by the state;
11 (3) Communication line costs arising from data and word processing
12 pursuant to subdivisions (1) and (2) of this section; and
13 (4) Multi-track recorders, microphones, and playback units used to
14 create verbatim records of county court proceedings.
15 (2) The county shall pay any county court expense not provided for
16 in this section. All property purchased by the county as a county court
17 expense before September 9, 1993, or on or after September 9, 1993, shall
18 remain the property of the county.
19 Sec. 2. Original section 24-514, Reissue Revised Statutes of
20 Nebraska, is repealed.
