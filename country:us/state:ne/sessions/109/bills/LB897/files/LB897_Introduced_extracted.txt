1 Section 1. Section 81-520.02, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska,
2 is amended to read:
3 81-520.02 (1) Any person violating the statewide open burning ban
4 established by section 81-520.01 or violating sections 81-520.03 to
5 81-520.05 shall be guilty of a Class III IV misdemeanor.
6 (2) In addition to the penalty provided in subsection (1) of this
7 section, a municipality or rural or suburban fire protection district may
8 charge a fee to such person to cover the costs incurred by a municipality
9 or rural or suburban fire protection district in responding to and
10 extinguishing any fire resulting from a violation of sections 81-520.01
11 or 81-520.03 to 81-520.05. Municipalities and rural and urban fire
12 protection districts may establish a fee structure consistent with the
13 approximate cost for responding to and extinguishing such fire.
14 Sec. 2. Original section 81-520.02, Reissue Revised Statutes of
15 Nebraska, is repealed.
