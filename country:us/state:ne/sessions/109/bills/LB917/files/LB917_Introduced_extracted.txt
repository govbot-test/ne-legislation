LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
SECOND SESSION
Introduced by Conrad, 46.
Read first time January 09, 2026
Committee: Executive Board
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the University of Nebraska; to amend
2 section 85-105, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change
3 requirements for the disposition of property as prescribed; and to
4 repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB917 LB917
2026 2026
1 Section 1. Section 85-105, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 85-105 The Board of Regents shall have full power to appoint its own
4 presiding officer and secretary. It shall constitute a body corporate, to
5 be known as the Board of Regents of the University of Nebraska, and as
6 such may sue and be sued and may make and use a common seal and alter the
7 same at pleasure. It may acquire real and personal property for the use
8 of the university and may dispose of the same whenever the university can
9 be benefited thereby, except that it shall never dispose of grounds upon
10 which a building of the university having a market value in excess of one
11 million dollars is located without a majority vote of the Board of
12 Regents in a public meeting authorizing the university to dispose of such
13 property the consent of the Legislature.
14 Sec. 2. Original section 85-105, Reissue Revised Statutes of
15 Nebraska, is repealed.
