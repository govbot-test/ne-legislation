2 Sec. 2. (1) The University of Nebraska State Museum License Plate
3 Cash Fund is created and shall be administered by the Board of Regents of
4 the University of Nebraska. The fund shall include money credited
5 pursuant to section 60-3,163.02, gifts, grants, bequests, private
6 contributions, and money from any public or private source. Any money in
7 the fund available for investment shall be invested by the state
8 investment officer pursuant to the Nebraska Capital Expansion Act and the
9 Nebraska State Funds Investment Act.
10 (2) Money in the fund shall be used for promoting understanding,
11 appreciation, and stewardship of science and natural history through
12 research, public education, and outreach at museums managed by the
13 University of Nebraska.
