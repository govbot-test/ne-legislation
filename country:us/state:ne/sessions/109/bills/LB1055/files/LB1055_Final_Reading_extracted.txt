1 Section 1. Section 28-348, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 28-348 Sections 28-348 to 28-387 and sections 3 and 4 of this act

5 Sec. 2. Section 28-350, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
6 amended to read:
7 28-350 For purposes of the Adult Protective Services Act, unless the
8 context otherwise requires, the definitions found in sections 28-351 to
9 28-371 and section 3 of this act shall be used.
10 Sec. 3. Adult protective services official means an individual who
11 is employed by the department and who develops and implements adult
12 protective services.
13 Sec. 4. (1) The department shall require each adult protective
14 services official to successfully complete training relating to caring
15 for individuals with Alzheimer's disease and other dementia. Such
16 training shall be no less than one hour in length. An adult protective
17 services official employed by the department on the effective date of
18 this act shall complete the training within thirty days of such date.
19 (2) The training required by subsection (1) of this section shall
20 include, but not be limited to: (a) Psychiatric and behavioral symptoms
21 of Alzheimer's disease and other dementia; (b) methods of communicating
22 respectfully and effectively with individuals with Alzheimer's disease
23 and other dementia; (c) techniques for understanding and approaching
24 behavioral symptoms; (d) information relating to safety including, but
25 not limited to, wandering; (e) alerting law enforcement agencies to
26 potential criminal behavior by a family member, a caretaker, or an
27 institution; (f) alerting law enforcement agencies to neglect or
28 exploitation; (g) the most common types of abuse; (h) methods of
29 identifying incidents of self-neglect; (i) methods of identifying neglect
30 by a caregiver; and (j) protocols for referral of such individuals to
31 local care resources and professionals who are skilled in Alzheimer's
