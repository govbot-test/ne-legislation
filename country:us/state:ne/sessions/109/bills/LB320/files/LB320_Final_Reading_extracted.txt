1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to human trafficking; to amend sections
2 81-1430 and 81-1431, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025; to require
3 hotels and similar public lodging establishments to place posters
4 relating to human trafficking in such hotels and establishments; to
5 provide for training about human trafficking to employees of such
6 hotels and establishments; to provide powers to the Department of
7 Labor and the Attorney General; to limit the liability of owners,
8 operators, and employees of such hotels or establishments relating
9 to human trafficking committed by a third party; to harmonize
10 provisions; and to repeal the original sections.

9 Sec. 3. (1) For purposes of this section:
10 (a) Hotel means a facility in which the public may, for a
11 consideration, obtain sleeping accommodations. Hotel includes hotels,
12 motels, tourist homes, campgrounds, courts, lodging houses, inns, state-
13 operated hotels, and nonprofit hotels but does not include hospitals,
14 sanitariums, nursing homes, long-term care facilities, or dormitories or
15 facilities operated by an educational institution and regularly used to
16 house students; and
17 (b) Human trafficking means labor trafficking, labor trafficking of
18 a minor, sex trafficking, or sex trafficking of a minor as those terms
19 are defined in section 28-830.
20 (2) Any person, firm, or corporation that owns or operates a hotel
21 or similar public lodging establishment may:
22 (a) Provide to all employees of such hotel or public lodging
23 establishment within the State of Nebraska training on human trafficking
24 that has been approved pursuant to subsection (2) of section 81-1431;
25 (b) Implement procedures for the reporting of suspected human
26 trafficking to the Nebraska Human Trafficking Hotline or to a local
27 enforcement agency; and
28 (c) Implement a human trafficking prevention policy for employees of
29 the hotel or establishment within the State of Nebraska.
30 (3) An owner, operator, or employee of a hotel or similar public
31 lodging establishment who implements the training and policies as
