2 Sec. 2. (1) The Back the Blue Cash Fund is created and shall be
3 administered by the Nebraska State Patrol. The fund shall consist of
4 gifts, grants, bequests, money received from any public or private
5 source, money credited pursuant to section 60-3,163.02, and money
6 transferred to the fund by the Legislature.
7 (2) Any money in the fund available for investment shall be invested
8 by the state investment officer pursuant to the Nebraska Capital
9 Expansion Act and the Nebraska State Funds Investment Act. Any interest
10 or realized capital gains from any such investment shall be credited to
11 the Back the Blue Cash Fund.
12 (3)(a) The fund shall only be used pursuant to this section.
13 (b) The Nebraska State Patrol may award grants from the fund
14 pursuant to section 3 of this act.
15 (c) The fund shall not be used for buying any equipment for or
16 paying any salary or cost for the Nebraska State Patrol.
17 Sec. 3. (1) For purposes of this section:
18 (a) Eligible purpose means:

20 (A) Injured law enforcement personnel;
21 (B) Spouse of any injured or deceased law enforcement personnel; or
22 (C) Child of any injured or deceased law enforcement personnel if
23 such child is under the age of nineteen years old; or
24 (ii) Conducting any charitable activity that supports the residents
25 of this state; and
26 (b) Qualified applicant means any association that represents
27 personnel of any law enforcement agency in this state.
28 (2) A qualified applicant may apply to the Nebraska State Patrol for
29 a grant under this section. The Nebraska State Patrol shall prescribe the
30 form for the application.
