LEGISLATIVE BILL 60
Approved by the Governor February 9, 2026
Introduced by Quick, 35.
A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the State Lottery Act; to amend section 9-829,
Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to remove the prohibition that a
lottery ticket cannot be sold through a vending or dispensing device; and
to repeal the original section.
Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
Section 1. Section 9-829, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is amended
to read:
9-829 A lottery game retailer shall sell tickets only on the premises
stated in the contract. No ticket shall be sold over a telephone or through the
mail. No credit shall be extended by the lottery game retailer for the purchase
of a ticket. No lottery tickets shall be sold through a vending or dispensing
device.
Sec. 2. Original section 9-829, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
repealed.
