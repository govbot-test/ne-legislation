1 Section 1. Beginning with school year 2027-28, each school
2 district, in consultation with the State Department of Education, shall
3 include instruction on the history of communism within the social studies
4 curriculum. Such instruction shall be age-appropriate and include:
5 (a) The history of communism in the United States, including
6 domestic communist movements and their associated histories and tactics;
7 (b) Atrocities committed in foreign countries in the name of
8 communism;
9 (c) Comparative discussion of political ideologies, such as
10 communism and totalitarianism, that conflict with the principles of
11 freedom and democracy essential to the founding principles of the United
12 States' representative republic system;
13 (d) The increasing threat of communism in the United States and its
14 allies through the 20th century, the events of the Cultural Revolution in
15 the People's Republic of China, the history of the Soviet Union, and the
16 mass killings that have occurred under communist regimes;
17 (e) The suppression and persecution of various religious faiths
18 under communist regimes; and
19 (f) The economic, industrial, and political events that have
20 preceded communist revolutions.
