Section 1:
LB838A LB838A
2026 2026
Approved by the Governor April 14, 2026
Introduced by Jacobson, 42.
A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid in
carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 838, One Hundred Ninth
Legislature, Second Session, 2026; and to declare an emergency.
Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,

Section 2:
Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $523,247 from the General
Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $136,300 from the General Fund for FY2027-28 to the
Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in carrying out the provisions
of Legislative Bill 838, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems from
funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $99,500 for FY2026-27 or
$102,500 for FY2027-28.
Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when passed and
approved according to law.
