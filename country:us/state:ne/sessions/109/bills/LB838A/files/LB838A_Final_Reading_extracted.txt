1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 838, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026; and to declare an
4 emergency.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB838A LB838A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $523,247 from the
2 General Fund for FY2026-27 and (2) $136,300 from the General Fund for
3 FY2027-28 to the Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in
4 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 838, One Hundred Ninth
5 Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $99,500
8 for FY2026-27 or $102,500 for FY2027-28.
9 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
