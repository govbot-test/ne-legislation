1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to first responders; to amend sections

6 protections; to include correctional officers, youth detention

10 provide duties for the Coordinating Commission for Postsecondary
11 Education and postsecondary educational institutions; to define and
12 redefine terms; to harmonize provisions; to repeal the original
13 sections; and to outright repeal sections 85-2603 and 85-2603.01,
14 Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska.
