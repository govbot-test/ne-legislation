1 Section 1. Sections 1 to 8 of this act shall be known and may be
2 cited as the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot Investment Act.
3 Sec. 2. The purpose of the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot
4 Investment Act is to partner with the manufacturing sector by providing
5 one-time grants to eligible manufacturing operations to assist such
6 operations with capital investments in qualified investments that will
7 increase, and lead to the adoption and integration of, smart technologies
8 and increased productivity in existing manufacturing operations located
9 in this state and that will provide a positive economic impact in the
10 State of Nebraska.
11 Sec. 3. For purposes of the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot
12 Investment Act:
13 (1) Department means the Department of Economic Development;
14 (2) Eligible manufacturing operation means a manufacturer of goods
15 at a facility located in this state that:
16 (a) Has a North American Industry Classification System number
17 within the manufacturing sector range of 31 to 33;
18 (b) Has been an established business for a minimum of three years
19 prior to the date of application for the grant;
20 (c) Derives a minimum of fifty-one percent of the manufacturer's
21 gross revenue from the sale of manufactured goods;
22 (d) Employs a minimum of three full-time employees; and
23 (e) Demonstrates the ability to provide private matching financial
24 support for the manufacturer's manufacturing modernization investment
25 project on a one-to-one basis;
26 (3) Grant means a manufacturing modernization investment grant
27 issued under the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot Investment Act in an
28 amount up to, but not to exceed, two hundred fifty thousand dollars from
29 the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot Investment Fund;
30 (4) Manufacturing modernization investment project means a project
31 that is intended to lead to the adoption and integration of smart

1 technologies into existing manufacturing operations located in the state
2 by mitigating the risk to the manufacturer of significant technology
3 investments. Manufacturing modernization investment project includes, but
4 is not limited to, an investment in specialized hardware, software, or
5 other equipment intended to assist a manufacturer in increasing the
6 manufacturer's productivity, efficiency, and competitiveness; and
7 (5) Private matching financial support means any financial support

10 Sec. 4. (1) Beginning October 1, 2025, through November 1, 2025, an
11 eligible manufacturing operation may apply to the department for a grant.
12 The application shall include, but not be limited to, the following
13 information:
14 (a) A description of the manufacturing modernization investment
15 project;
16 (b) The estimated cost of the manufacturing modernization investment
17 project; and
18 (c) Documentation on the amount of private matching financial
19 support available for the manufacturing modernization investment project
20 that have been received or will be received by the eligible manufacturing
21 operation. Such amount shall be at least equal to the amount of any grant
22 received under the act. The documentation provided under this subdivision
23 does not need to identify any provider of private matching financial
24 support.
25 (2) The department shall consider applications in the order in which
26 they are received. If an applicant is an eligible manufacturing operation
27 and otherwise qualifies for a grant, the department shall, subject to
28 subsection (3) of this section, approve the application and notify the
29 applicant of the approval.
30 (3) The department may approve applications within the limits of
31 available funding under the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot Investment

6 Sec. 5. (1) The department shall not approve an application for a
7 manufacturing modernization investment project that was commenced prior
8 to the date of the application.
9 (2) Any eligible manufacturing operation that no longer meets the

13 Sec. 6. The Manufacturing Modernization Pilot Investment Fund is
14 created. The fund shall consist of transfers authorized by the
15 Legislature and any gifts, grants, or bequests from any source, including
16 federal, state, public, and private sources. The fund shall be
17 administered by the department and shall be used to provide grants under
18 the Manufacturing Modernization Pilot Investment Act. Any money in the
19 fund available for investment shall be invested by the state investment
20 officer pursuant to the Nebraska Capital Expansion Act and the Nebraska
21 State Funds Investment Act.
