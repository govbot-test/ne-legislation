1 Section 1. Section 53-201, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 53-201 The purpose of sections 53-201 to 53-223 and section 3 of
4 this act is to provide fair, efficient, and competitive distribution of
5 beer by (1) regulating the termination, expiration, and renewal of
6 distribution agreements between beer suppliers and beer wholesalers, (2)
7 promoting a distribution system in which each beer wholesaler will devote
8 reasonable efforts and resources to sales, distribution, and quality
9 control of the beer it sells, (3) promoting the continued availability of
10 good quality beer for the consumers of Nebraska through orderly marketing
11 and vigorous interbrand competition, (4) preventing a beer supplier from
12 unfairly depriving a beer wholesaler of the value of the investment the
13 wholesaler made in its business in terms of money, time, effort, and
14 skill, and (5) controlling the sale of malt beverages in this state and
15 facilitating the lawful and orderly marketing of malt beverages pursuant
16 to the police powers of this state.
