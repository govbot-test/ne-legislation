1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to public health and welfare; to define terms;
2 to require public schools, public postsecondary educational
3 institutions, and state agencies to designate restrooms and locker
4 rooms based on sex and prohibit use by the opposite sex as
5 prescribed; and to require each state agency to define an
6 individuals sex as either male or female for purposes of rules and
7 regulations, the enforcement of administrative actions, and the
8 adjudication of disputes as prescribed.
9 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,

1 Section 1. (1) For purposes of this section:
2 (a) Boy means an adolescent human male;

12 and a girl;

23 (i) Woman means an adult human female.

28 governing body of a public school shall not:
