1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the Motor Vehicle Certificate of Title Act;
2 to amend sections 60-101, 60-137, 60-152, 60-164, and 60-166,
3 Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to allow applications for
4 certificates of title for certain vehicles; to change provisions

6 certain conveyances, and evidence of liens; to provide for the
7 perfection and priority of security interests in and the surrender
8 and acquisition of certificates of title of certain vehicles; to
9 harmonize provisions; and to repeal the original sections.
