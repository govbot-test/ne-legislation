LB632 LB632
2025 2025
1 Section 1. (1) The Legislature declares that the purpose of this
2 section is to protect the public health and welfare and to encourage and
3 express respect for the dignity of unborn children by providing that
4 health care facilities performing elective abortions provide for the
5 dignified and safe disposition of the remains of aborted unborn children.
6 (2) For purposes of this section, remains of an aborted unborn child
7 means the physical remains of an unborn child at any stage of gestation
8 whose life has been terminated by an elective abortion.
9 (3) It shall be unlawful for a health care facility at which an
10 elective abortion has been performed to deposit or dispose of the remains
11 of an aborted unborn child in a manner other than provided in this
12 section.
13 (4) A health care facility at which an elective abortion has been
14 performed shall provide for the individual disposition of the remains of
15 the aborted unborn child by cremation or interment by burial or, if not
16 possible, as directed by the Board of Health.
17 (5) No notice of the method of disposition shall be required to be
18 given to a woman upon whom an abortion was performed.
