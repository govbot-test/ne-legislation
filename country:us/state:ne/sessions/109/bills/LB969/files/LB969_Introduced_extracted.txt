1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to political subdivisions; to provide for the

1 Section 1. (1) The Auditor of Public Accounts shall develop,
2 maintain, and make available for public inspection on its website a
3 database of financial information from all counties in the state. The
4 information required shall include, but not be limited to, revenue
5 sources, expenditures, and a balance sheet that contains all assets and
6 liabilities for each county from the most recent audit reports filed with
7 the Auditor of Public Accounts pursuant to section 23-1608. The Auditor
8 of Public Accounts shall designate an implementation date for such
9 database which date is on or before January 1, 2027.
10 (2)(a) Counties shall provide to the Auditor of Public Accounts such
11 information as is necessary to develop and maintain the database.
12 Counties shall provide updated information to the Auditor of Public
13 Accounts on an annual basis, and the Auditor of Public Accounts shall
14 update the database no later than thirty days after receiving any such
15 information.
16 (b) Counties shall provide historical financial information
17 beginning with calendar year 2016 to the Auditor of Public Accounts. Such
18 information shall be collected and added to the database if available as
19 soon as practicable.
20 (3) Nothing in this section requires the disclosure of information
21 which is considered confidential under state or federal law or is not a
22 public record under section 84-712.05.
23 Sec. 2. (1) The Auditor of Public Accounts shall develop, maintain,
24 and make available for public inspection on its website a database of
25 financial information from all cities of the metropolitan class in the
26 state. The information required shall include, but not be limited to,
27 revenue sources, expenditures, and a balance sheet that contains all
28 assets and liabilities for each city of the metropolitan class's most
29 recent municipal audit. The Auditor of Public Accounts shall designate an
30 implementation date for such database which date is on or before January
31 1, 2027.
