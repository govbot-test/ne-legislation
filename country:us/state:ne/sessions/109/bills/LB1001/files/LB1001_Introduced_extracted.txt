1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to racing and gaming; to amend sections
2 2-1207.01, 2-1213, 2-1216, 2-1226, 2-1228, 2-1229, 9-831, 9-1001,
3 9-1002, 9-1003, 9-1004, 9-1006, and 9-1115, Reissue Revised Statutes
4 of Nebraska, section 2-1207, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
5 2024, and sections 2-1205, 2-1210, 9-1,101, and 9-1104, Revised
6 Statutes Supplement, 2025; to change provisions relating to licensed
7 racetrack enclosure terms and conditions, parimutuel wagering, the
8 distribution of amounts deducted from wagers on horseracing,
9 registration of Nebraska-bred horses, simulcast facilities licenses,
10 and assistance to problem gamblers; to provide for administrative
11 fees; to change provisions relating to the Nebraska Commission on
12 Problem Gambling and place such commission for administrative
13 purposes within the State Racing and Gaming Commission; to change
14 and eliminate provisions relating to the Compulsive Gamblers
15 Assistance Fund; to change provisions relating to the Charitable
16 Gaming Division of the Department of Revenue; to provide an
17 exception to the required age for individuals to play keno at a
18 licensed racetrack enclosure; to harmonize provisions; to repeal the
19 original sections; and to outright repeal section 9-1007, Reissue
20 Revised Statutes of Nebraska.
21 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
