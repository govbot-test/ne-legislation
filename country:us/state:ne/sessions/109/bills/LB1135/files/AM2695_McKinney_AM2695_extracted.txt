AMENDMENTS TO LB1135
Introduced by McKinney, 11.
1 1. On page 19, line 26, strike "and" and show as stricken; and after
2 line 28 insert the following new subdivision:
3 "(vii) At least one member who resides in a qualified census tract
4 located within the municipality that created the land bank. If there is
5 more than one qualified census tract within such municipality, the member
6 shall reside within whichever qualified census tract contains the most
7 land bank property. If there is no qualified census tract within such
8 municipality, then this subdivision shall not apply. For purposes of this
9 subdivision, qualified census tract has the same meaning as in 26 U.S.C.
10 42(d)(5)(B)(ii)(I), as such section existed on January 1, 2026; and".
