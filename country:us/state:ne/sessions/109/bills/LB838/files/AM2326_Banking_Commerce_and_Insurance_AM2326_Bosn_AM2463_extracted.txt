1 Sec. 5. The Legislature finds and declares that:
2 (1) The United States has determined that the governments of China,
3 Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Russia, and the Maduro Regime in Venezuela are

5 long-term patterns or serious instances of conduct significantly adverse
6 to United States national security or the security and safety of United
7 States persons;
8 (2) China's effort to advance its technological capabilities poses
9 significant threats to the United States and its citizens;
10 (3) China is currently operating a Military-Civil Fusion strategy
11 with the goal of developing the most technologically advanced military in
12 the world. A key part of this strategy is removing barriers between
13 China's civilian sectors and its military and defense industrial sectors;

15 international markets;
