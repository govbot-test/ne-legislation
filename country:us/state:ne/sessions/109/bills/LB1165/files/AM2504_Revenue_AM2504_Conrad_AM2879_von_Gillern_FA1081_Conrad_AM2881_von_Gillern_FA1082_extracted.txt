3 Section 1. Sections 1 to 12 of this act shall be known and may be
4 cited as the Grow the Good Life Act.
5 Sec. 2. The purpose of the Grow the Good Life Act is to provide
6 incentives to encourage large in-state employers to retain workforce and
7 their headquarters in-state, as well as attract or relocate workforce to
8 the state, when there is a material change in ownership or control
9 pursuant to a merger or business combination with a company located
10 outside of this state.

19 Nebraska Act;

24 period of at least ten years prior to the date of the merger or
25 combination;
