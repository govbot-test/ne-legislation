1 Section 1. Sections 1 to 12 of this act shall be known and may be
2 cited as the Grow the Good Life Act.
3 Sec. 2. The purpose of the Grow the Good Life Act is to provide
4 incentives to encourage large in-state employers to retain workforce and
5 their headquarters in-state, as well as attract or relocate workforce to
6 the state, when there is a material change in ownership or control
7 pursuant to a merger or business combination with a company located
8 outside of this state.

17 Nebraska Act;
