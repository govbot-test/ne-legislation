4 amended to read:
5 60-6,175 (1) Upon meeting or overtaking, from the front or rear, any
6 school bus on which the yellow warning signal lights are flashing, the
7 driver of a motor vehicle shall reduce the speed of such vehicle to not
8 more than twenty-five miles per hour, shall bring such vehicle to a
9 complete stop when the school bus is stopped, the stop signal arm is
10 extended, and the flashing red signal lights are turned on, and shall
11 remain stopped until the flashing red signal lights are turned off, the
12 stop signal arm is retracted, and the school bus resumes motion. This
13 section shall not apply to approaching traffic in the opposite direction
14 on a divided highway or to approaching traffic when there is displayed a
15 sign as provided in subsection (8) of this section directing traffic to
16 proceed. Any person violating this subsection shall be guilty of a Class
17 IV misdemeanor, shall be fined five hundred dollars, and shall be
18 assessed points on his or her motor vehicle operator's license pursuant
19 to section 60-4,182.
20 (2) Except as provided in subsection (8) of this section, the driver
21 of any school bus, when stopping to receive or discharge pupils, shall
22 turn on flashing yellow warning signal lights at a distance of not less
23 than three hundred feet when inside the corporate limits of any city or
24 village and not less than five hundred feet nor more than one thousand
25 feet in any area outside the corporate limits of any city or village from
26 the point where such pupils are to be received or discharged from the
27 bus. At the point of receiving or discharging pupils, the bus driver
28 shall bring the school bus to a stop, extend a stop signal arm, and turn
29 on the flashing red signal lights. After receiving or discharging pupils,
30 the bus driver shall turn off the flashing red signal lights, retract the
31 stop signal arm, and then proceed on the route.
