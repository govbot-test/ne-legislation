LB201 LB201

1 Section 1. Section 8-2701, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 8-2701 Sections 8-2701 to 8-2747 and section 2 of this act shall be
4 known and may be cited as the Nebraska Money Transmitters Act.
5 Sec. 2. (1) A licensee or authorized delegate shall collect a fee
6 for each money transmission transaction sent to a person outside the
7 United States. The fee shall be in an amount equal to (a) ten percent of
8 the value of the money transmission transaction or (b) ten thousand
9 dollars, whichever is less.
10 (2) The fee required by subsection (1) of this section shall be
11 remitted quarterly to the department with a form prescribed by the
12 department with the assistance of the Nebraska State Patrol. All fees and
13 forms shall be remitted to the department not later than the fifteenth
14 day of the month following the close of each calendar quarter.
15 Sec. 3. This act becomes operative on January 1, 2026.
16 Sec. 4. Original section 8-2701, Reissue Revised Statutes of
17 Nebraska, is repealed.
