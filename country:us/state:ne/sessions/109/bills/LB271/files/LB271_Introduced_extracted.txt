6 Sec. 2. (1) For purposes of this section:
7 (a) Interfere means to tamper with, displace, remove, damage,
8 disable, destroy, set fire to, impair, or otherwise interfere with
9 something; and
10 (b) Rail infrastructure means any of the following that are located
11 on railroad property or are owned, leased, possessed, operated, or
12 otherwise used for or in connection with railroad operations: A train,
13 locomotive, freight or passenger car, or any other on-track vehicle or
14 equipment; any railroad track or structure; any signalling or
15 communication system or component; or any station, terminal, depot, or
16 other facility.
17 (2) A person commits an offense if such person knowingly, willfully,
18 or recklessly, and without lawful authority or permission:
19 (a) Places any obstruction upon or within or otherwise interferes
20 with any rail or any part of the tracks, ties, switches, ballast, or
21 other fixture or device, or the securement thereof, or any bridge,
22 tunnel, viaduct, tower, culvert, drain, trestle, embankment, parapet, or
23 other structure or any part thereof, attached to or connected to or used
24 with such tracks or the operation of any railroad;
25 (b) Interferes with any signal, mechanism, device, equipment,
26 bungalow, communication lines or antennas, or other appurtenance used in
27 or with, or controlling the signal or communication systems of, any
28 railroad, including any train control system, centralized dispatching
29 system, wayside detector, communication tower, buried or aerial facility,
30 or highway-rail grade crossing warning system;
31 (c) Interferes with, or places or releases a biological agent or
