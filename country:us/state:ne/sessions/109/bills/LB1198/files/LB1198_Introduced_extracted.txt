1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to ticket sales; to require paper tickets for
2 certain events as prescribed; and to provide penalties.
3 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-

2026 2026
1 Section 1. (1) For purposes of this section:
2 (a) Large public event means an event held at any indoor or outdoor
3 facility that can accommodate at least five thousand people; and
4 (b) Paper ticket means a physical document used for the purpose of
5 granting admission to an event.
6 (2) Any public or private entity selling electronic tickets to a
7 large public event shall provide a paper ticket, free of charge, to any
8 ticket purchaser upon request. The paper ticket shall be delivered to the
9 purchaser prior to the event or made available for printing by the
10 purchaser prior to the event.
11 (3) The Attorney General shall enforce this section. Any public or
12 private entity that violates this section shall be subject to a civil
13 penalty in an amount equal to ten dollars multiplied by the total number
14 of tickets sold at the event at which the violation occurred. All civil
15 penalties collected pursuant to this section shall be remitted to the
16 State Treasurer for distribution in accordance with Article VII, section
17 5, of the Constitution of Nebraska.
