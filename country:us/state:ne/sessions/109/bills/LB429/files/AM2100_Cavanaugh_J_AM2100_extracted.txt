3 Section 1. (1) For purposes of this section:
4 (a) Certificated employee has the same meaning as in section 79-824;

6 (b) Professional employees' organization means any organization that
7 engages in one or more of the following activities: Providing liability
8 protection or collective bargaining on behalf of certificated employees.
9 (2) A school board shall grant access upon a request made by a
10 professional employees' organization to the physical or electronic
11 mailbox of any certificated employee.
12 (3) A professional employees' organization, in an effort to recruit
13 new members, is permitted to display information at or send a
14 representative who either is an employee of the school district or
15 represents an employee of the school district to attend a certificated
16 employee meeting or event, including, but not limited to, a certificated
17 employee orientation meeting.
18 (4) If a professional employees' organization is allowed to create
19 and post signage or materials in a school such as an information board, a
20 bulletin board, a poster, or a pamphlet, any other professional
21 employees' organization shall also be granted permission to create and
22 post signage and materials in an equal manner.
23 (5) A school board shall not designate any day or break in the
24 school calendar by naming or referring to the day or break using the name
25 of any professional employees' organization.
26 (6) Nothing in this section shall be construed to apply to, modify,
27 supersede, or affect in any way the provisions of the Industrial
