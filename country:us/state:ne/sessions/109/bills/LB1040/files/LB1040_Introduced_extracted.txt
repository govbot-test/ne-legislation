1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to civil commitments; to amend sections
2 71-908, 71-921, 71-923, 71-925, 71-930, 71-932, and 71-949, Reissue
3 Revised Statutes of Nebraska, and sections 71-901, 71-903, 71-922,
4 and 81-1850, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025; to change
5 requirements for commitment as a mentally ill and dangerous person
6 under the Nebraska Mental Health Commitment Act; to define and
7 redefine terms; to allow interested parties to file petitions under
8 the act; to change provisions relating to inpatient commitment; to
9 provide a penalty for a petition filed in bad faith; to provide
10 duties for mental health boards and county attorneys; to harmonize
11 provisions; and to repeal the original sections.
12 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
