1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated $4,000,000 from the General
2 Fund for FY2025-26 to the Military Department, for Program No. 545,
3 Emergency Management, for a grant for equipment for emergency rescue
4 events. To be eligible for a grant, the political subdivision shall show
5 to the Military Department that it is capable of assisting in statewide
6 rescue events. The grant shall be used exclusively to purchase equipment,
7 including, but not limited to, equipment that assists in emergency rescue
8 events such as water rescue events, equipment that aides in searching a
9 wide area such as tornado and flood situations, equipment used in
10 structural collapses, and equipment for hazardous materials situations.
11 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
12 passed and approved according to law.
