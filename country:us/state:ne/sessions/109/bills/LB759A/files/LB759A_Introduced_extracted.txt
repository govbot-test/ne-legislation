LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
SECOND SESSION
Introduced by Brandt, 32.
Read first time March 09, 2026
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 759, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB759A LB759A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $415,000 from the
2 Department of Water, Energy, and Environment Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and
3 (2) $415,000 from the Department of Water, Energy, and Environment Cash
4 Fund for FY2026-27 to the Department of Water, Energy, and Environment,
5 for Program 334, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative
6 Bill 759, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
7 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $150,000 from the
8 Livestock Waste Management Cash Fund and $75,000 from the Integrated
9 Solid Waste Management Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $150,000 from the
10 Livestock Waste Management Cash Fund and $325,000 from the Integrated
11 Solid Waste Management Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Department of
12 Water, Energy, and Environment, for Program 513, to aid in carrying out
13 the provisions of Legislative Bill 759, One Hundred Ninth Legislature,
14 Second Session, 2026.
15 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
16 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
17 section.
