1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to fees; to provide a duty for each state
2 agency to examine fees charged.
3 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB776 LB776
2026 2026
1 Section 1. (1) Each year beginning January 1, 2027, each state
2 agency shall examine any fee charged and determine whether the fee is
3 sufficient, but not exceeding the amount necessary, to cover costs that
4 are intended to be covered by the fee.
5 (2) For purposes of this section, state agency means any department,
6 agency, board, bureau, or commission of the state.
