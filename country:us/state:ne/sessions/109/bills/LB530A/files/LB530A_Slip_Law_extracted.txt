LEGISLATIVE BILL 530A
Approved by the Governor June 4, 2025
Introduced by Bosn, 25.
A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid in
carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 530, One Hundred Ninth
Legislature, First Session, 2025.
Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $134,000 from the Supreme
Court Automation Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $-0- from the Supreme Court
Automation Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Supreme Court, for Program 570, to
aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 530, One Hundred Ninth
Legislature, First Session, 2025.
No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems for
state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this section.
