1 Section 1. Section 38-1201, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
2 2024, is amended to read:
3 38-1201 Sections 38-1201 to 38-1239 and section 2 of this act shall
4 be known and may be cited as the Emergency Medical Services Practice Act.
5 Sec. 2. (1) An emergency care provider shall not be eligible to
6 receive state-funded emergency medical services grant assistance
7 administered by the department unless such provider certifies, on a form
8 prescribed by the department, that the provider maintains and implements
9 a policy for billing for services by submitting claims to public or
10 private third-party payors, if appropriate.
11 (2) For purposes of this section, billing for services means the
12 regular submission of a claim for reimbursement to medicare, medicaid, a
13 private health insurer, or a legally responsible third-party payor for
14 emergency or non-emergency medical services provided by the emergency
15 care provider.
16 Sec. 3. Original section 38-1201, Revised Statutes Cumulative
17 Supplement, 2024, is repealed.
