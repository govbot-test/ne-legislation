2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $4,750 from the State

4 Program 549, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill
5 1127, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, Second Session, 2026.

7 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
8 section.
