1 Section 1. Sections 1 to 10 of this act shall be known and may be
2 cited as the Agricultural Data Privacy Act.
3 Sec. 2. The Legislature finds that agricultural data is a
4 proprietary business asset that originates from the farm, land, devices,
5 and equipment of agricultural producers. To protect the economic value of
6 this data and the autonomy of Nebraska producers, the sale of such data
7 shall be protected and the security of such data shall be maintained
8 through reasonable safeguards.
