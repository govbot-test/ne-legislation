AMENDMENTS TO LB794
Introduced by DeKay, 40.
1 1. Insert the following new section:
3 amended to read:
4 2-3232 Each district shall have the power and authority to:
5 (1) Make studies, investigations, or surveys and do research as may
6 be necessary to carry out its authorized purposes, enter upon any land,
7 after notifying the owner or occupier, for the purpose of conducting such
8 studies, investigations, surveys, and research, and publish and
9 disseminate the results. Entry upon any property pursuant to this section
10 shall not be considered trespass, and damages are not recoverable on that
11 account alone. In case of any actual or demonstrable damage to premises,
12 the district shall pay the owner of the premises the amount of the
13 damages. Upon failure of the landowner and the district to agree upon the
14 amount of damages, the landowner, in addition to any other available
15 remedy, may file a petition as provided in section 76-705. To avoid
16 duplication of effort, any such studies, investigations, surveys,
17 research, or dissemination shall be in cooperation and coordination with
18 the programs of the University of Nebraska, or any department thereof,
19 and any other appropriate state agencies; and
20 (2) Conduct demonstration projects within the district on lands
21 owned or controlled by this state or any of its agencies, with the
22 cooperation of the agency administering and having jurisdiction thereof,
23 and on any other lands within the district, upon obtaining the consent of
24 the owners of such land or the necessary rights and interest in such
25 lands, in order to demonstrate by example the means, methods, and
26 measures by which soil and water resources may be conserved and soil
27 erosion in the form of soil blowing and soil washing may be prevented and
