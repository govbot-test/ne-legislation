1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; to amend section 60-3,191,

LB207 LB207
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 60-3,191, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
2 2024, is amended to read:
3 60-3,191 (1) In addition to any other fee required under the Motor
4 Vehicle Registration Act, a fee for registration of each motor vehicle
5 powered by an alternative fuel shall be charged. The fee shall be:
6 (a) For one hundred fifty dollars, except that for a motorcycle or
7 plug-in hybrid electric vehicle, the fee shall be seventy-five dollars; .
8 (b) For any commercially registered alternative fuel vehicle having
9 a gross weight of more than seven thousand five hundred pounds, three
10 times the applicable fee for other motor vehicles powered by an
11 alternative fuel as described in subdivision (1)(c) of this section; or
12 (c) For any other motor vehicle powered by an alternative fuel, one
13 hundred fifty dollars.
14 (2) The fee shall be collected by the county treasurer and remitted
15 to the State Treasurer for credit to the Highway Trust Fund.
16 Sec. 2. Original section 60-3,191, Revised Statutes Cumulative
17 Supplement, 2024, is repealed.
