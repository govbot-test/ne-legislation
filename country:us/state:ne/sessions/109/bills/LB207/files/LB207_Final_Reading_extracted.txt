1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; to amend section 60-3,191,
2 Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement, 2024; to provide a fee for
3 certain commercially registered alternative fuel vehicles; and to
4 repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB207 LB207
2026 2026
1 Section 1. Section 60-3,191, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
2 2024, is amended to read:
3 60-3,191 (1) In addition to any other fee required under the Motor
4 Vehicle Registration Act, a fee for registration of each motor vehicle
5 powered by an alternative fuel shall be charged. The fee shall be:
6 (a) For one hundred fifty dollars, except that for a motorcycle or
7 plug-in hybrid electric vehicle, the fee shall be seventy-five dollars; .
8 (b) For any commercially registered alternative fuel vehicle that is
9 not registered pursuant to section 60-3,198 and that has a gross weight
10 of more than seven thousand five hundred pounds, three times the
11 applicable fee for other motor vehicles powered by an alternative fuel as
12 described in subdivision (1)(c) of this section; or
13 (c) For any other motor vehicle powered by an alternative fuel, one
14 hundred fifty dollars.
15 (2) The fee shall be collected by the county treasurer and remitted
16 to the State Treasurer for credit to the Highway Trust Fund.
17 Sec. 2. Original section 60-3,191, Revised Statutes Cumulative
18 Supplement, 2024, is repealed.
