E AND R AMENDMENTS TO LB 1235
1 1. On page 1, strike beginning with "medical" in line 1 through line
2 18 and insert "public health and welfare; to amend sections 53-103.47 and
3 53-1,100, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, section 53-132, Revised
4 Statutes Cumulative Supplement, 2024, and sections 53-123.17, 71-24,106,
5 and 71-24,109, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025; to change provisions of
6 the Nebraska Liquor Control Act relating to bottle clubs, licensure
7 determinations, and penalties for conducting certain activities without a
8 license; to redefine a term; to provide for a consumption-only class of
9 entertainment district license; to change the Nebraska Medical Cannabis
10 Regulation Act to provide salaries for members of the Nebraska Medical
11 Cannabis Commission, create a fund, provide for rules and regulations
12 relating to fees, and provide for criminal history record information
13 checks; to harmonize provisions; to provide operative dates; to repeal
14 the original sections; and to declare an emergency.".
