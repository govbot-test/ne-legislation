7 burning ban under subsection (1) of this section for an area under the

9 a person requesting permission to conduct open burning. The permit issued
10 by the fire chief to a person desiring to conduct open burning shall at a
11 minimum contain (a) the name and telephone number of the landowner, (b)

20 form with the minimum requirements on the website of the State Fire

23 burning ban in the local fire department's jurisdiction when conditions
24 are acceptable to the chief. Anyone intending to burn in such
25 jurisdiction when the open burning ban has been waived shall notify the
26 fire chief of his or her intention to burn prior to starting the burn.
27 (4) The fire chief of a local fire department may adopt standards
28 listing the conditions acceptable for issuing a permit to conduct open
