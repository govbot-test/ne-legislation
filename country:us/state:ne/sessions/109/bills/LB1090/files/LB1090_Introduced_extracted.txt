1 Section 1. Section 32-629, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025, is
2 amended to read:
3 32-629 For any petition prepared or filed pursuant to the Election
4 Act:
5 (1) Each petition signer shall be a registered voter and qualified
6 to sign a petition at the time of signing a petition, except that a
7 signer of a petition for President of the United States, a candidate
8 petition, a new political party petition, or an initiative or referendum
9 petition under sections 32-1401 to 32-1409 shall be a registered voter of
10 the State of Nebraska on or before the date on which the petition is
11 filed with the filing officer; and
12 (2) Only a person who (a) is at least eighteen years of age and (b)
13 has not been convicted of a felony or pleaded guilty to a felony unless
14 such person has received a pardon for any such felony shall qualify as a
15 valid circulator of a petition and may circulate petitions. Petition
16 circulators shall comply with section 32-630; and .
17 (3) Each petition circulator shall file a notarized affidavit with
18 the Secretary of State prior to circulating petitions stating:
19 (a) The petition circulator's name and date of birth;
20 (b) The petition circulator's mailing address;
21 (c) Whether the petition circulator has been convicted of a felony
22 or pleaded guilty to a felony; and
23 (d) Whether the petition circulator has received a pardon for any
24 felony such petition circulator has been convicted or pleaded guilty.
25 Sec. 2. Section 32-1546, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025, is
26 amended to read:
27 32-1546 (1) Any person who is not, at the time of signing a
28 petition, a registered voter and qualified to sign the petition except as
29 provided in subdivision (1) of section 32-629 or who signs any name other
30 than his or her own to any petition shall be guilty of a Class I
31 misdemeanor.
