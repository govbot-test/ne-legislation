1 Section 1. Section 72-1240, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 72-1240 The council shall appoint a state investment officer. The
4 appointment shall be subject to the approval of the Governor and a
5 majority of the Legislature. The state investment officer shall be a
6 person well qualified by training to administer and invest the money
7 available for investment, and he or she shall have at least five years of
8 experience in the management of investment portfolios. The state
9 investment officer's salary shall be fixed by the council at not less
10 than twenty-five thousand dollars per year. The state investment officer
11 shall serve without term and may be removed for cause by the council or
12 the Governor.
13 Sec. 2. Original section 72-1240, Reissue Revised Statutes of
14 Nebraska, is repealed.
