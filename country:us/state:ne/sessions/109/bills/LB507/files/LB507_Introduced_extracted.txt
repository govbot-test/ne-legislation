3 79-1129 (1) The school board of the resident school district shall

6 (a) Provide for the transportation expenses for children with
7 disabilities who are forced to leave the school district temporarily
8 because of lack of educational services. A parent or guardian
9 transporting such a child shall be paid for each day of attendance at the
10 mileage rate provided in section 81-1176 for each actual mile or fraction
11 thereof traveled between the place of residence and the program of
12 attendance, and when any parent or guardian transports more than one
13 child with a disability in his or her custody or control enrolled in

15 guardian shall be based upon the transportation of one such child. No
16 transportation payments shall be made to a parent or guardian for mileage
17 not actually traveled by such parent or guardian;
18 (b) Provide for the transportation expenses within the school
19 district of any child with a disability enrolled in the school district

21 either (i) the child is required to attend a facility other than what
22 would be the normal school or attendance facility of the child to receive
23 appropriate special educational services or (ii) the nature of the
24 child's disability is such that special transportation is required. A
25 parent or guardian transporting such child shall be paid for each day of
26 attendance at the mileage rate provided in section 81-1176 for each
27 actual mile or fraction thereof traveled between the place of residence
28 and the program of attendance, and when any parent or guardian transports
