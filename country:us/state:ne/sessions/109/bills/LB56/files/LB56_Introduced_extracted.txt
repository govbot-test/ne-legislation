1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to law enforcement; to amend section 60-6,102,
2 Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to require medical facilities
3 and mortuaries to comply with requests for blood draws relating to
4 certain deceased persons as prescribed; and to repeal the original
5 section.

LB56 LB56

1 Section 1. Section 60-6,102, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska,
2 is amended to read:
3 60-6,102 (1) In the case of a driver who dies within four hours
4 after being in a motor vehicle accident, including a motor vehicle
5 accident in which one or more persons in addition to such driver is
6 killed, and of a pedestrian sixteen years of age or older who dies within
7 four hours after being struck by a motor vehicle, the coroner or other
8 official performing the duties of coroner shall examine the body and
9 cause such tests to be made as are necessary to determine the amount of
10 alcohol or drugs in the body of such driver or pedestrian.
11 (2) Such information shall be included in each report submitted
12 pursuant to sections 60-6,101 to 60-6,104 and shall be tabulated on a
13 monthly basis by the Department of Transportation. Such information,
14 including the identity of the deceased and any such amount of alcohol or
15 drugs, shall be public information and may be released or disclosed as
16 provided by the department.
17 (3) A medical facility or mortuary shall comply with a request from
18 a peace officer for a blood draw of a driver or pedestrian described in
19 subsection (1) of this section.
20 Sec. 2. Original section 60-6,102, Reissue Revised Statutes of
21 Nebraska, is repealed.
