LEGISLATIVE BILL 98
Introduced by Moser, 22.

Committee: Transportation and Telecommunications
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; to amend sections 60-103,
2 60-135.01, 60-305, 60-358.01, and 60-6,355, Reissue Revised Statutes
3 of Nebraska; to redefine the definitions of all-terrain vehicle and
4 utility-type vehicle in the Motor Vehicle Certificate of Title Act,
5 the Motor Vehicle Registration Act, and the Nebraska Rules of the
6 Road; to repeal the original sections; and to declare an emergency.
7 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
