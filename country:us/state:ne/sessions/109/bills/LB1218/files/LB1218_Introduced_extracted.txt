1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the County Bridge Match Program; to amend
2 sections 39-2805 and 66-4,100, Revised Statutes Cumulative
3 Supplement, 2024; to require the County Bridge Match Working Group
4 to award money as prescribed; to change provisions of the Highway
5 Cash Fund and the Roads Operations Cash Fund; to provide for
6 transfers of money from the Roads Operations Cash Fund; to eliminate
7 obsolete provisions; and to repeal the original sections.
8 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,

2026 2026
1 Section 1. Section 39-2805, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
2 2024, is amended to read:
3 39-2805 (1) The County Bridge Match Program is created. The
4 department shall administer the program using funds from the
5 Transportation Infrastructure Bank Fund. The purpose of the program is to
6 promote innovative solutions and provide additional funding to accelerate
7 the repair and replacement of deficient bridges on the county road
8 system. Participation by counties in the program shall be voluntary.
9 (2)(a) (2) The County Bridge Match Working Group is hereby created.
10 The Governor shall appoint two representatives to serve on the working
11 group from a list of county highway superintendents, county surveyors, or
12 county engineers submitted by a statewide association representing county
13 officials. The Director-State Engineer shall select three representatives
14 of the department to serve on the working group. The working group shall
15 develop the program, including participation criteria and matching fund
16 requirements for counties, and shall score the applications and award the
17 funds. A meeting of the working group does not constitute a meeting as
18 defined in section 84-1409.
19 (b) The working group shall award at least four million dollars to
20 applicants each fiscal year under the County Bridge Match Program.
21 (3) The County Bridge Match Program terminates on June 30, 2029.
22 Sec. 2. Section 66-4,100, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
23 2024, is amended to read:
24 66-4,100 (1) The Highway Cash Fund and the Roads Operations Cash
25 Fund are hereby created. If bonds are issued pursuant to subsection (2)
26 of section 39-2223, the balance of the share of the Highway Trust Fund
27 allocated to the Department of Transportation and deposited into the
28 Highway Restoration and Improvement Bond Fund as provided in subsection
29 (8) of section 39-2215 and the balance of the money deposited in the
30 Highway Restoration and Improvement Bond Fund as provided in section
31 39-2215.01 shall be transferred by the State Treasurer, on or before the
