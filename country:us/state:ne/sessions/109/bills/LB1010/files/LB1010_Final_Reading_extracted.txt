7 (2) Onsite backup generating facilities means (a) generation of one
8 megawatt or larger that is not capable of operating in parallel with the
9 grid for greater than one-tenth of a second or (b) generation of one
10 megawatt or larger that is capable of operating continuously in parallel
11 with the grid, non-exporting, and meeting applicable generator
12 interconnection requirements; and
13 (3) Public power supplier means a public power district organized

18 the Joint Public Agency Act, an agency formed under the Municipal
19 Cooperative Financing Act, or any other governmental entity providing
20 electric service.
