2026 2026
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
SECOND SESSION
FINAL READING
Introduced by Kauth, 31.
Read first time January 07, 2026
Committee: Judiciary
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to law enforcement; to authorize law
2 enforcement agencies to obtain, maintain, and possess epinephrine
3 autoinjectors and nasal epinephrine for use by a law enforcement
4 officer as prescribed; to require training; and to provide powers
5 and duties to the Department of Health and Human Services.
6 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB727 LB727
2026 2026
1 Section 1. (1) For purposes of this section:
2 (a) Authorized forms of epinephrine means an epinephrine
3 autoinjector or nasal epinephrine;
4 (b) Administer means the direct application of an authorized form of
5 epinephrine to the body of an individual;
6 (c) Department means the Department of Health and Human Services;
7 (d) Epinephrine autoinjector means a single-use device used for the
8 automatic injection of a premeasured dose of epinephrine into the human
9 body;
10 (e) Law enforcement agency means the police department or town
11 marshal in incorporated municipalities, the office of the sheriff in
12 unincorporated areas, and the Nebraska State Patrol;
13 (f) Law enforcement officer means any member of the Nebraska State
14 Patrol, any county or deputy sheriff, any member of the police force of
15 any city or village, or any other public official authorized by a city or
16 village to enforce state or local laws, rules, regulations, or
17 ordinances; and
18 (g) Nasal epinephrine means a needle-free, prescription nasal spray
19 containing epinephrine intended for the emergency treatment of allergic
20 reactions.
21 (2) Subject to the requirements of this section and any rules and
22 regulations of the department:
23 (a) A law enforcement agency may obtain, maintain, and possess
24 authorized forms of epinephrine to be provided to or made available to
25 such agency's law enforcement officers pursuant to this section for use
26 while in the performance of official duties; and
27 (b) A law enforcement officer may administer authorized forms of
28 epinephrine in response to an emergency situation while in the
29 performance of such officer's official duties.
30 (3) The department shall, in consultation with law enforcement
31 agencies, approve one or more educational training programs relating to
-2-
LB727 LB727
2026 2026
1 responding to an anaphylaxis event, including the use of authorized forms
2 of epinephrine, that law enforcement officers may complete in order to be
3 provided and administer authorized forms of epinephrine pursuant to this
4 section. Any such program shall provide a law enforcement officer with a
5 certificate of completion for successful completion of such program.
6 (4)(a) A law enforcement agency shall not provide or make available
7 authorized forms of epinephrine to a law enforcement officer until such
8 officer has provided such law enforcement agency with a copy of a
9 certificate of completion of a program described in subsection (3) of
10 this section.
11 (b) A law enforcement officer shall complete an approved educational
12 training program pursuant to subsection (3) of this section and provide a
13 certificate of completion to the officer's agency prior to administering
14 authorized forms of epinephrine in the performance of such officer's
15 official duties.
16 (5) Nothing in this section shall require:
17 (a) A law enforcement agency to obtain authorized forms of
18 epinephrine or make such authorized forms of epinephrine available to law
19 enforcement officers; or
20 (b) A law enforcement officer to carry or administer authorized
21 forms of epinephrine.
22 (6) The department may issue a standing order authorizing law
23 enforcement agencies to purchase authorized forms of epinephrine.
24 (7) The department may adopt and promulgate rules and regulations to
25 carry out this section.
