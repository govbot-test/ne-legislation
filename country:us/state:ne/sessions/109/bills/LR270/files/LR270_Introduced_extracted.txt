ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 270
Introduced by Guereca, 7; Juarez, 5.
WHEREAS, the National Defense Act of 1916 established the Reserve Officer
Training Corps (ROTC) and the Junior Reserve Officer Training Corps (JROTC);
and
WHEREAS, ROTC and JROTC programs serve communities, provide military
leadership, and motivate youth to become better citizens; and
WHEREAS, the JROTC from Omaha South High School in Omaha, Nebraska, is
sponsored by Sergeant Sorenson; and
WHEREAS, the Omaha South High JROTC competed in the Battalion Regional
Raider Competition in Black Hills, South Dakota; and
WHEREAS, the Omaha South High JROTC placed first in overall raider,
obstacle course, physical endurance course, cross county rescue, 5K team run,
and rope bridge; and
WHEREAS, the Omaha South High JROTC placed second in the gauntlet; and
WHEREAS, the Omaha South High JROTC won first place overall at the
Battalion Regional Raider Competition.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST SESSION:
1. The Legislature congratulates the students of the Omaha South High
School JROTC for earning first place at the Battalion Regional Raider
Competition.
2. That a copy of this resolution be sent to the Omaha South High School
JROTC.
