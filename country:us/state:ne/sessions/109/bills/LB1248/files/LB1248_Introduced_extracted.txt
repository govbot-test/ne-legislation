2026 2026
1 Section 1. Laws 2025, LB261, section 280, is amended to read:
2 Sec. 280. AGENCY NO. 84 — DEPARTMENT OF WATER, ENERGY, AND
3 ENVIRONMENT
4 Program No. 513 - Environmental Quality
5 FY2025-26 FY2026-27
6 GENERAL FUND 5,593,323 43,593,323
7 GENERAL FUND 5,593,323 5,593,323
8 CASH FUND 42,816,431 43,115,996
9 FEDERAL FUND est. 45,798,301 46,175,320
10 PROGRAM TOTAL 94,208,055 132,884,639
11 PROGRAM TOTAL 94,208,055 94,884,639
12 SALARY LIMIT 18,821,791 19,216,295
13 The Department of Administrative Services shall monitor the
14 appropriations and expenditures for this program according to the
15 following program classifications:
16 No. 513 - Administration
17 No. 586 - Water Quality
18 No. 587 - Land Quality
19 No. 588 - Air Quality
20 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
21 $18,645,601 Cash Funds and $24,538,513 Federal Funds estimate for state
22 aid, which shall only be used for such purpose. There is included in the
23 appropriation to this program for FY2026-27 $38,000,000 General Funds,
24 $18,645,601 Cash Funds, and $24,529,499 Federal Funds estimate for state
25 aid, which shall only be used for such purpose.
26 The unexpended Cash Fund appropriation balance appropriated to the
27 Department of Natural Resources for this program existing on June 30,
28 2025, up to $6,000,000, is hereby reappropriated for providing grants to
29 metropolitan utilities districts for lead service line replacements in
30 accordance with section 71-5328.
31 There is included in the amount shown as aid for this program
-2-
LB1248 LB1248
2026 2026
1 $38,000,000 General Funds for FY2026-27 for the purpose of awarding a
2 grant to a city of the first class which operates a publicly owned
3 wastewater treatment facility that is subject to a National Pollutant
4 Discharge Elimination System permit and that has experienced documented
5 increases in industrial wastewater load resulting in treatment capacity
6 or compliance challenges due to a calcium caseinate food ingredient
7 manufacturing facility.
8 Sec. 2. Original Laws 2025, LB261, section 280, is repealed.
9 Sec. 3. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
