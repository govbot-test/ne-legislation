16 of money received pursuant to an application shall be limited to the uses

18 and indirect, and all grants on the website of the Department of Health
19 and Human Services.

21 utilization of such money to the Clerk of the Legislature annually
22 through December 31, 2032. The report shall include a list of all
23 beneficiaries that includes direct awards, indirect awards passed through
24 from the direct awards, and any outcomes and metrics collected.
