1 Section 1. Section 71-24,103, Revised Statutes Supplement, 2025, is
2 amended to read:
3 71-24,103 Sections 71-24,103 to 71-24,105 and section 2 of this act
4 shall be known and may be cited as the Nebraska Medical Cannabis Patient
5 Protection Act.
6 Sec. 2. A health care practitioner shall not be subject to arrest,
7 prosecution, or penalty, in any manner, or denied any right or privilege,
8 including, but not limited to, a civil penalty or disciplinary action by
9 the Department of Health and Human Services or by any other occupational
10 or professional licensing board, solely for providing a written
11 recommendation or for stating that, in the health care practitioner's
12 professional opinion, a patient is likely to receive therapeutic or
13 palliative benefit from using cannabis to treat or alleviate a patient's
14 medical condition.
15 Sec. 3. Original section 71-24,103, Revised Statutes Supplement,
16 2025, is repealed.
