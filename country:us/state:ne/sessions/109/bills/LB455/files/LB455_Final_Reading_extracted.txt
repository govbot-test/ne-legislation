15 48-146.03 (1) Each workers' compensation insurance policy issued by

24 insured employer's otherwise applicable annual workers' compensation
25 insurance premium at rates approved for the insurer but not less than
26 fifty thousand dollars.
27 The insured employer, if choosing to exercise one of such options
28 listed in this subsection, may choose only one of the amounts as the
29 deductible. The provisions of this section shall be fully disclosed to
30 each prospective purchaser in writing.
