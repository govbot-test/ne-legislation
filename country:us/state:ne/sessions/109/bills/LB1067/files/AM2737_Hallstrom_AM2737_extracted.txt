AMENDMENTS TO LB1067
Introduced by Hallstrom, 1.
1 1. Strike section 1 and insert the following new section:
2 Section 1. Section 58-703, Revised Statutes Cumulative Supplement,
3 2024, is amended to read:
4 58-703 (1) The Affordable Housing Trust Fund is created. The fund
5 shall receive money pursuant to section 76-903 and may include revenue
6 from sources recommended by the housing advisory committee established in
7 section 58-704, appropriations from the Legislature, transfers authorized
8 by the Legislature, grants, private contributions, repayment of loans,
9 and all other sources. The Department of Economic Development as part of
10 its comprehensive housing affordability strategy shall administer the
11 Affordable Housing Trust Fund.
12 (2)(a) Prior to July 1, 2027, transfers Transfers may be made from
13 the Affordable Housing Trust Fund to the General Fund, the Behavioral
14 Health Services Fund, the Lead-Based Paint Hazard Control Cash Fund, the
15 Middle Income Workforce Housing Investment Fund, the Rural Workforce
16 Housing Investment Fund, and the Site and Building Development Fund at
17 the direction of the Legislature.
18 (b) Beginning on and after July 1, 2027, money shall not be
19 transferred from the Affordable Housing Trust Fund.
