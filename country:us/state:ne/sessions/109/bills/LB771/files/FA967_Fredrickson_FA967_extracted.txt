Floor Amendment To LB771
Introduced by Fredrickson, 20.
On page 3, strike section (iv) lines 1-10, and insert the following
"Dynamic pricing shall not be permitted for seven days after any state of
emergency declared by the Governor. The prohibition shall be confined to the
areas delineated in the Governor's emergency proclamation. The Governor may
further regulate dynamic pricing at his discretion during any state of
emergency through executive order."
