LB841A LB841A
2026 2026
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $118,000 from the

4 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 841, One Hundred Ninth
5 Legislature, Second Session, 2026.
6 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
7 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
8 section.
