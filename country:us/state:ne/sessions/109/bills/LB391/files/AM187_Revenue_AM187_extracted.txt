3 Section 1. Sections 1 to 5 of this act shall be known and may be
4 cited as the Give to Enable Support Act.
5 Sec. 2. For purposes of the Give to Enable Support Act:
6 (1) Account has the same meaning as in section 77-1401;
7 (2) Contribution means a donation which is made for the purpose of
8 providing a source of funding for the program;
9 (3) Program means the Give to Enable Support Program created in
10 section 4 of this act;
11 (4) Qualified disability expenses has the same meaning as in section
12 77-1401; and
13 (5) Qualified individual means an individual with a disability as
14 defined in section 77-1401 who does not have an account at the time he or
15 she is approved to become a participant in the program pursuant to
16 section 4 of this act.
17 Sec. 3. There is hereby created the Give to Enable Support Cash
18 Fund. The fund shall be administered by the State Treasurer. The State
19 Treasurer shall accept contributions from any private individual or
20 private entity and shall credit all such contributions received to the
21 Give to Enable Support Cash Fund for the purpose of providing an ongoing
22 source of funding for the program. No General Funds shall be transferred
23 to the Give to Enable Support Cash Fund. Any money in the fund available
24 for investment shall be invested by the state investment officer pursuant
25 to the Nebraska Capital Expansion Act and the Nebraska State Funds
26 Investment Act.
27 Sec. 4. (1) The Give to Enable Support Program is created. The
